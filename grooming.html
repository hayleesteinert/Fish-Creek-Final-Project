Great Grooming

Keep your pet looking clean and neat with our grooming services. Bring your dog or cat in today for a full grooming including:

  • Bath (with shampoo and conditioner)
  • Toe nail trimming
  • Anal gland expression for dogs
  • Ear cleaning
  • Flea and tick examination, and treatment if needed
  • Checks for skin conditions
    • If found, they can be brought to the attention of our veterinarian who can recommend the proper shampoo
  • Certified stylists will perform:
    • Summer cuts
    • Lion cuts
    • Breed specific cuts
    • Other specialty clips
