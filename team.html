Dr. Haylee Steinert

Dr. Haylee Steinert is a Burleson High School graduate and has recently settled here in Arlington. She graduated from veterinary school in 2001 from Texas A&M University and then did an internship at the I-20 Animal Emergency Clinic.

Dr. Steinert currently works relief locally and enjoys enjoys tennis, gardening and playing piano.

Dr. Sang Hyun Park

Dr. Sang Hyun Park is an Associate Veterinarian at Fish Creek Animal Clinic. Since joining the team in June of 2015, he has focused on administering care to animals that visit our animal hospital through preventative medicine, dentistry, surgery and diagnostic services. Dr. Park has particular interest in feline medicine, and plans to pursue an ABVP certification in feline medicine after five years in practice.

In his free time, he enjoys reading, attending concerts, hiking, backpacking, and traveling.

Dr. Malik Bahhur

Dr. Malik Bahhur has been a veterinarian for over 30 years. Dr. Bahhur received his doctorate in veterinary medicine from Kansas State University and has also completed additional training in small animal cardiology, veterinary dentistry, and feline medicine. He has done many studies on rodents.

In his free time, he likes to work out, watch movies, and spend time with his family.
